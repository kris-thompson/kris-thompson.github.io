About Me

Hi! My name is Kris Thompson, thank you for taking the time to view my site. Here I will give you a bit of background on myself and what I'm currently up to.


Skills and Education

I graduated from the University of Iowa in May of 2014 with a Bachelors in Informatics. During my time in college I used various different programming languages such as Java, SQL, and HTML to name a couple. I was also working in retail at Target while going through college and have years of customer service experience as well as experience working in team-based/solo environments and working with deadlines from my time there. I also have experience with computer hardware from making custom PCs on my own time.


Hobbies and Interests

Occasionally I also do some woodworking when I have the tools available, and when I have time at home I generally will be playing video games or working on some programming projects which I will be adding information about on this site as they become more developed.
